Protocol boundaries

Fund Registry is a non-custodial publishing tool. By using this service, you acknowledge the following:

🛡
Non-custodialWe never hold, receive, or transmit funds. Bitcoin is sent directly to the address published by the page creator.
↩
Irreversible transactionsBitcoin transactions cannot be reversed, refunded, or disputed. Verify the address and the campaign before sending.
✓
Verification scopeWallet verification proves cryptographic control of the listed address. It does not verify identity, intent, or truthfulness.
🔑
Campaign Key ownershipAccess to a campaign page is controlled by a downloaded Campaign Key file. Fund Registry cannot recover it, but access can be restored by signing a wallet challenge with the funding address.
⏳
Time-bound pagesAll pages expire. Free pages last 10 days. Paid pages last 30 or 90 days. After expiry, pages enter a grace period and then are tombstoned.
🔒
Immutable Bitcoin addressThe listed Bitcoin address cannot be changed after page creation. Fund Registry is Bitcoin-only. This is a security measure to prevent address-swap attacks.

For page creators

  • You are solely responsible for the accuracy of your page description.
  • You must control the Bitcoin address you publish. Wallet verification is required for paid tiers.
  • You must download and securely store your Campaign Key. Fund Registry cannot recover it, but you can regain access via wallet signature.
  • Pages that violate applicable law or are reported as fraudulent may be removed.

For donors

  • Verify the campaign and address before sending any Bitcoin.
  • A green verification badge means the creator proved wallet control — not that the campaign is legitimate.
  • Transactions are final. Fund Registry cannot reverse, refund, or mediate payments.
  • Do your own due diligence. We are a registry, not an escrow or intermediary.
